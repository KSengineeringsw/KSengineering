About K&S Engineering

Mechanics
You Can Trust

"We treat every vehicle like it belongs to one of our own. No upselling. No guesswork. Just proper mechanical work done properly."

K&S Engineering has been part of the Bideford community for over two decades. We started as a small workshop taking on anything and everything, and that attitude hasn't changed — whether it's an MOT on a city runabout or a complex diagnostic on a modern performance car.

Our team of fully qualified technicians works across all makes and models, domestic and European. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment so we can solve modern problems without the main dealer price tag.
